Sales Report Q1 2024

Regional Performance:
North region achieved sales of 1.5 million USD in Q1 2024.
South region reported sales of 0.8 million USD.
East region grew by 20 percent compared to last quarter.
West region faced challenges due to supply chain issues.

Top Products:
Laptop sales contributed 45 percent of total revenue.
Mobile phones accounted for 30 percent of sales.
Tablets showed declining trend with only 10 percent contribution.

Credit Underwriting Policy:
Credit underwriting is the process of evaluating loan applications.
The bank assesses income, credit score, and collateral before approving loans.
Minimum credit score required is 700 for personal loans.
Debt to income ratio must be below 40 percent for loan approval.
