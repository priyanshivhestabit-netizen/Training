Employee Handbook 2024

Onboarding Process:
New employees must complete onboarding within first 30 days.
ID card and system access will be provided on day one.
Mandatory training modules must be completed within 2 weeks.

Benefits:
Health insurance is provided to all full-time employees and their families.
Provident fund contribution is 12 percent of basic salary.
Annual bonus is paid in March based on company and individual performance.

IT Policy:
Company laptops must not be used for personal activities.
All software installations require IT department approval.
VPN must be used when accessing company systems remotely.
