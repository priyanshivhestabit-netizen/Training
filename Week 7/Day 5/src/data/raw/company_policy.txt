Company HR Policy Document 2024

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year.
Sick leave is limited to 10 days per year with medical certificate.
Maternity leave is 26 weeks as per government regulations.

Work Hours:
Standard work hours are 9 AM to 6 PM Monday to Friday.
Remote work is allowed up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Code of Conduct:
All employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will lead to termination.
Confidential company data must not be shared outside the organization.

Performance Review:
Performance reviews are conducted every 6 months.
Employees are rated on a scale of 1 to 5.
Increment is based on performance rating and market conditions.
